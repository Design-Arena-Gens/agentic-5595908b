Questions, answered

How does FuseNotes keep my client information private?
All workspaces are isolated, with encryption at rest and in transit using TLS 1.3. Access controls let you invite collaborators with role-based permissions, and every export is logged for audit trails.
Can I import past meeting notes or recordings?
Yes. Batch upload docs, transcripts, or audio files and FuseNotes will generate summaries and action items retroactively so your knowledge base starts full from day one.
Do you support compliance requirements?
We provide data residency options, SOC2-ready policies, and can sign NDAs upon request. Enterprise security reviews are available with annual contracts.
What integrations are available?
FuseNotes connects with Zoom, Google Meet, Slack, Notion, Asana, and Zapier. More integrations are on the roadmap—reach out if you have a priority request.
